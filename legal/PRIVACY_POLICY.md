# Privacy Policy for Sleep Architect

**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025

## Introduction

Sleep Architect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address (if you create an account)
- **Sleep Data:** Sleep duration, sleep quality scores, wake-up times, sleep patterns
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, preferences

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage:** Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Sleep Tracker service
- Track and analyze your sleep patterns
- Provide personalized sleep recommendations
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Send notifications (with your permission)
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage
- We do NOT send your personal data to external servers
- Your sleep data remains on your device unless you explicitly export it
- Account credentials are stored securely on your device only

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Transfer your data to external servers (unless you use export feature)

We MAY share information only:
- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your stored data
- **Export:** Download all your data at any time (via Settings > Export My Data)
- **Delete:** Permanently delete your account and all data (via Settings > Delete Account)
- **Portability:** Take your data with you

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Local storage encryption
- Secure authentication practices
- Regular security updates
- No transmission of sensitive data over networks

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Third-Party Services

### Expo Platform
Our app is built with Expo, which may collect analytics data. See Expo's privacy policy at: https://expo.dev/privacy

### No Other Third Parties
We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:
- **Vibration:** For alarm and notification alerts
- **Wake Lock:** To keep screen active during sleep tracking
- **Notifications:** To send sleep reminders and alarms (optional)

We DO NOT use:
- Camera
- Microphone (unless explicitly needed for sleep recording feature)
- Location services
- Contacts

## Data Retention

- Data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- Exported data is under your control
- Deleted accounts and data cannot be recovered

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Notifying you via in-app message (for significant changes)

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: asadalibscs20@gmail.com
- App: Settings > Help & Support

## Consent

By using Sleep Tracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
